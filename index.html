Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

We provide drivers for all kinds of vehicles, including cars, vans, trucks, and buses. Our drivers are experienced and licensed, and have undergone background checks to ensure their suitability for the role.

We have a rigorous screening process that includes background checks, driving record checks, and drug tests. We only work with drivers who meet our high standards for professionalism and safety.

Our drivers have all the necessary qualifications and licenses required to drive the vehicles they are assigned to. They also undergo regular training to ensure they are up-to-date with the latest driving regulations and safety standards.

We understand that businesses may need drivers at short notice, and we strive to provide drivers as quickly as possible. We work with a large pool of qualified drivers, which allows us to match businesses with suitable drivers in a timely manner.

We strive to provide the best possible match for each business, and we take into account specific driver preferences when making assignments. However, we cannot guarantee the availability of specific drivers.

If a business is not satisfied with a driver for any reason, we will work to find a suitable replacement as quickly as possible. We also welcome feedback from businesses to help us improve our driver services.
